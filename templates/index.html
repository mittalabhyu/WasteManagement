WASTE MANAGEMENT

"An initiative to clean our city"

SERVICES

  • Request for dustbin in your area
  • Send picture of your dirty locality
  • Complain if the sweeper has not come.
  • Tell your problem directly to your councilor
  • Report if the drainage system is blocked.
  • Complain id dustbin in your area is full and no vehicle has come to collect waste.

VISION

  • Waste management is a need of todays world as due to increasing population the waste generated is also increasing.
  • Our systems are under tremendous pressure and are not able to handle the waste.
  • The main problem is lack of coordination, tedious methods to register complains, corruption at various levels etc.
  • This online system provides a platform for common people to address their issues directly to the concerned authorities.
